MEDICAL REFERENCE GUIDE
Diseases, Medicines, and Dosages

===========================================

DIABETES MELLITUS TYPE 2

Primary Medications:
- Metformin: 500mg twice daily with meals, maximum 2000mg/day
- Glipizide: 5mg once daily before breakfast, maximum 20mg/day
- Insulin Glargine: 10-20 units subcutaneous once daily at bedtime
- Sitagliptin: 100mg once daily with or without food

===========================================

HYPERTENSION (HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE)

Primary Medications:
- Lisinopril: 10mg once daily, maximum 40mg/day
- Amlodipine: 5mg once daily, maximum 10mg/day
- Hydrochlorothiazide: 25mg once daily in morning
- Metoprolol: 50mg twice daily, maximum 200mg/day

===========================================

DEPRESSION

Primary Medications:
- Sertraline: 50mg once daily, maximum 200mg/day
- Fluoxetine: 20mg once daily in morning, maximum 80mg/day
- Escitalopram: 10mg once daily, maximum 20mg/day
- Bupropion: 150mg twice daily, maximum 450mg/day

===========================================

ANXIETY DISORDERS

Primary Medications:
- Alprazolam: 0.25mg three times daily, maximum 4mg/day
- Lorazepam: 1mg twice daily, maximum 6mg/day
- Buspirone: 15mg twice daily, maximum 60mg/day
- Clonazepam: 0.5mg twice daily, maximum 4mg/day

===========================================

ASTHMA

Primary Medications:
- Albuterol Inhaler: 2 puffs every 4-6 hours as needed
- Fluticasone Inhaler: 2 puffs twice daily
- Montelukast: 10mg once daily in evening
- Prednisone: 40mg daily for 5 days (acute exacerbation)

===========================================

PNEUMONIA

Primary Medications:
- Amoxicillin: 500mg three times daily for 7-10 days
- Azithromycin: 500mg on day 1, then 250mg daily for 4 days
- Levofloxacin: 750mg once daily for 5 days
- Ceftriaxone: 1g IV once daily for severe cases

===========================================

URINARY TRACT INFECTION

Primary Medications:
- Nitrofurantoin: 100mg twice daily for 5 days
- Trimethoprim-Sulfamethoxazole: 160/800mg twice daily for 3 days
- Ciprofloxacin: 250mg twice daily for 3 days
- Fosfomycin: 3g single dose

===========================================

GASTROESOPHAGEAL REFLUX DISEASE (GERD)

Primary Medications:
- Omeprazole: 20mg once daily before breakfast
- Pantoprazole: 40mg once daily
- Ranitidine: 150mg twice daily
- Famotidine: 20mg twice daily

===========================================

MIGRAINE HEADACHE

Primary Medications:
- Sumatriptan: 50mg at onset, may repeat once after 2 hours
- Rizatriptan: 10mg at onset, maximum 30mg/day
- Propranolol: 80mg twice daily for prevention
- Topiramate: 25mg twice daily for prevention

===========================================

OSTEOARTHRITIS

Primary Medications:
- Ibuprofen: 400mg three times daily with food
- Naproxen: 220mg twice daily
- Acetaminophen: 650mg four times daily, maximum 3000mg/day
- Celecoxib: 200mg once daily

===========================================

RHEUMATOID ARTHRITIS

Primary Medications:
- Methotrexate: 15mg once weekly with folic acid
- Hydroxychloroquine: 200mg twice daily
- Sulfasalazine: 1000mg twice daily
- Prednisone: 5-10mg daily (lowest effective dose)

===========================================

HYPOTHYROIDISM

Primary Medications:
- Levothyroxine: 50-100mcg once daily on empty stomach
- Liothyronine: 25mcg twice daily
- Armour Thyroid: 60mg once daily

===========================================

HYPERTHYROIDISM

Primary Medications:
- Methimazole: 15mg once daily
- Propylthiouracil: 100mg three times daily
- Propranolol: 40mg twice daily for symptoms

===========================================

ATRIAL FIBRILLATION

Primary Medications:
- Warfarin: 5mg daily, adjust based on INR
- Apixaban: 5mg twice daily
- Metoprolol: 50mg twice daily
- Diltiazem: 120mg once daily

===========================================

HEART FAILURE

Primary Medications:
- Lisinopril: 10mg once daily, titrate up
- Metoprolol Succinate: 25mg once daily, titrate up
- Furosemide: 40mg once daily
- Spironolactone: 25mg once daily

===========================================

CHRONIC KIDNEY DISEASE

Primary Medications:
- Lisinopril: 10mg once daily
- Furosemide: 40mg once daily
- Calcium Carbonate: 1250mg three times daily with meals
- Epoetin Alfa: 50-100 units/kg three times weekly

===========================================

EPILEPSY

Primary Medications:
- Phenytoin: 300mg once daily
- Carbamazepine: 200mg twice daily
- Valproic Acid: 500mg twice daily
- Levetiracetam: 500mg twice daily

===========================================

PARKINSON'S DISEASE

Primary Medications:
- Carbidopa-Levodopa: 25/100mg three times daily

- Ropinirole: 2mg three times daily

===========================================

Primary Medications:
- Donepezil: 10mg once daily at bedtime
- Rivastigmine: 6mg twice daily

- Memantine: 10mg twice daily

===========================================

IMPORTANT NOTES:

1. All dosages are for adult patients unless specified
2. Dosages may need adjustment based on patient weight, age, and kidney function
3. Always check for drug interactions and allergies
4. Monitor patients for side effects and therapeutic response
5. Some medications require regular laboratory monitoring
6. Consult prescribing information for complete dosing guidelines

===========================================

CONTRAINDICATIONS AND WARNINGS:

- Metformin: Contraindicated in severe kidney disease
- ACE Inhibitors: Monitor potassium levels and kidney function
- NSAIDs: Use caution in patients with kidney disease or heart failure
- Warfarin: Requires regular INR monitoring
- Benzodiazepines: Risk of dependence and withdrawal
- Antibiotics: Complete full course even if symptoms improve
